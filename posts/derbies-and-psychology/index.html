Derbies & Psychology: When Data Meets Narrative

August 13, 2025

Derbies are noisy: motivation spikes, refereeing becomes harder, and game states swing faster. Our response:

  • Reduce pre‑match confidence and widen error bars.
  • Elevate card markets and unders when officiating is lenient.
  • Avoid over‑reacting to historical narratives—calibrated numbers first, story second.

Prediction is not about bravado; it’s about properly pricing uncertainty.
