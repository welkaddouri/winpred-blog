How to Read WinPred Cards: Confidence, Not Certainty

August 9, 2025

A quick guide to the elements on each match card:

  • Outcome pick: Home / Draw / Away—our highest probability outcome, not a guarantee.
  • Confidence bar: a band summarizing information richness and model agreement.
  • Price edge: the gap between our implied probability and the current market price (where available).
  • Top exact scores: the mode and the next two most probable scores to guide alternative markets.

Smart usage

  • Compare our implied probability to the odds you can actually get.
  • Scale stake size with confidence, not with emotion.
  • Track your own results per market; specialization beats generalization.

The card is a cockpit: fast, clear and honest about uncertainty.
