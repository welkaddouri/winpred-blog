League Breakdown: Where Our Model Shines (and Why)

August 5, 2025

Leagues are ecosystems. Styles, parity, officiating and schedules all shape predictability.

Stronger tiers

  • Liga Portugal, LaLiga, Brasileirão: stylistic clarity and larger gaps in squad quality. Teams that dominate territory and shot quality create repeatable edges; the market tends to underreact to fatigue and travel within these calendars.
  • Serie A: slightly lower scoring with tactical stability; our style matching performs well here.

Tougher tiers

  • Premier League & EFL Championship: deeper squads, higher parity, heavier rotation, and sharper markets. Bookmakers move fast on injuries and rest—edges decay quickly.

What we do about it

  • Better availability modeling (who actually starts matters more than who exists on paper).
  • Finer game‑state behavior (how teams defend a lead vs chase a goal).
  • League‑specific calibration to turn raw probabilities into well‑behaved predictions.

The goal is not perfection; it’s steady, explainable gains. Expect incremental improvements season over season, with transparent reporting.
