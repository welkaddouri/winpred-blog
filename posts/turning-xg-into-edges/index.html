Turning xG into Edges on Totals

August 15, 2025

We estimate expected goals for each side, but totals depend on variance:

  • Pressing duels create transitions → more shots in short windows.
  • Set‑piece quality turns low‑tempo games into scoring spikes.
  • Game state: early leads deflate pace; late chases inflate it.

Rules of thumb:

  • Over 2.5 needs more than 2.5 expected goals; we look for 2.6–2.8+ and conducive styles.
  • Unders thrive in low‑transition, compact matchups with limited set‑piece threat.
